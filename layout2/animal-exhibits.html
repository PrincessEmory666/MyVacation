Red Siamese Fighting Fish

This amazing fish is extremely territorial and will fight to the death if put in the same tank.

Facts about the fish
  • They are commonly known as a Beta.
  • They can't live in the same tank as their own species.
  • They were bred for their agression.
  • They are harmless to humans.



Sea Wasp Jellyfish

This species of jellyfish is teh most deadly in the world. If left untreated, the victim can die within three minutes of being stung

Facts about the fish:
  • Its tentacles can get up to 15 feet long
  • The "bell" of this animal can get to the size of a basketball.
  • It is the largest of the Box jellyfish.
  • They are known to have killed over 100 people.
